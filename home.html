Our path

Get glimpse of our milestones

  • 2016

    Platinum and Gold Award winning

    A big change of europen nature

    United Nations Organization (UNO) had awarded both platinum and gold award for focusing on nature, where SK International Group had planted nearly 720,000,000 million trees all over the europe.

  • 2015

    Europen Family Tree Project

    Evolution of tree from their parents had begun.

    SK Group had spend nearly 7 million Euro for the development of the project called Europen Family Tree, in which evey stakeholder family had voluntarily strted to plant a tree.

  • 2014

    International Plantation Aggreement

    Revolution of game change in nature.

    Our company had invited to sign a treaty with UNO for Europen plantion aggrement. Very next day of our aggrement, we had started to plant a tree in further Europen countries: Italy, Greece, Croatia, Spain, Sweden, Norway, Malta, Albania, Belgium, Bulgaria, Ireland, Finland, Serbian, Albania and finally to Iceland.

  • 2013

    DID IT

    More 701,679 trees were panted

    At the month of September 2013, SK Internationl Group had planeted more than 701,679 tree in England, Russia, Germany, Denmark, Netherland, Poland, Austria, France, Switzerland and Hungary.

  • 2012

    COMPANY FOUNDED

    A iconic mark of the company had founded

    SK International Group came with one tree project, which define every stakeholder must plant a tree. At 12th May conference in Netherland, it came to aggrement with every stakeholder to implement it.
